About Bridgee Solutions

Your Partner in Growth and Efficiency.

Who We Are

Founded in 2024 by Fiker Anteneh and Michael Nigussie, Bridgee Solutions was born from a shared vision to bridge the gap between global businesses seeking reliable, skilled outsourcing services and the rich pool of untapped talent in Ethiopia. With Fiker's extensive background in operations management and customer success, combined with Michael's deep expertise in technology and software development, Bridgee Solutions is uniquely positioned to offer a comprehensive suite of BPO and technology solutions.

We are more than just an outsourcing company; we are a dedicated partner committed to understanding your unique business needs and delivering tailored services that drive growth, efficiency, and innovation.

Our Journey

Our Humble Beginnings

Starting with a small but passionate team, Bridgee Solutions embarked on its mission to showcase Ethiopian talent on the world stage. Our initial focus was on providing exceptional virtual assistant services, quickly establishing a reputation for reliability and quality. As our clients' needs grew, so did our expertise.

Expansion & Commitment

We expanded into software development, leveraging local tech talent to build innovative solutions, and further diversified into custom BPO services, covering customer support, data entry, and more. Each milestone in our journey has been marked by a commitment to learning, adaptation, and exceeding expectations. We've invested heavily in training programs, technology infrastructure, and creating a supportive work environment that nurtures talent and fosters professional growth. Today, Bridgee Solutions stands as a testament to the power of vision, dedication, and the incredible potential within Ethiopia.
