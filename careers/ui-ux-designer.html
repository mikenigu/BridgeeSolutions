UI/UX Designer

Location: Addis Ababa, Ethiopia - Remote Friendly | Job Type: Full-time

Role Overview

We are seeking a talented and creative UI/UX Designer to craft intuitive, engaging, and visually appealing user interfaces and experiences for our web and mobile applications. You will be responsible for the entire design process, from conducting user research and creating wireframes to developing high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct user research, interviews, and usability testing to understand user needs and pain points.
  • Create user personas, journey maps, wireframes, and sitemaps to guide design decisions.
  • Develop high-fidelity mockups, visual designs, and interactive prototypes for web and mobile platforms.
  • Collaborate closely with developers, product managers, and other stakeholders to ensure design feasibility and consistency.
  • Maintain and evolve design systems and style guides.

Required Qualifications

  • Proven experience as a UI/UX Designer or similar role, with a strong portfolio showcasing your design work.
  • Proficiency in design and prototyping tools such as Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, or similar.
  • Solid understanding of user-centered design principles, usability, and accessibility best practices.
  • Excellent visual design skills with a keen eye for detail, typography, color, and layout.
  • Ability to effectively communicate design ideas and rationale to stakeholders.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience designing for responsive web applications and native mobile apps (iOS and Android).
  • Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and basic front-end development concepts.
  • Experience working in an agile development environment.

What We Offer (Benefits)

  • Competitive remuneration and benefits.
  • Flexible work options, including remote opportunities.
  • A creative and collaborative environment to grow your skills.
  • Opportunity to work on diverse projects and shape user experiences.
  • Access to industry-standard design tools and resources.

How to Apply

To apply, please submit your CV, a cover letter, and your portfolio (link or PDF) to careers@bridgeesolutions.com with the job title "UI/UX Designer" in the subject line.
