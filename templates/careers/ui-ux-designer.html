UI/UX Designer

Location: Remote/Hybrid | Job Type: Full-time

Role Overview

Bridgee Solutions is looking for a creative and passionate UI/UX Designer to join our dynamic team. You will be responsible for crafting intuitive, engaging, and visually appealing user experiences for a variety of web and mobile applications. The ideal candidate will have a strong portfolio showcasing user-centered design principles, a keen eye for detail, and a drive to create seamless and effective digital products.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create wireframes, storyboards, user flows, process flows, and site maps to effectively communicate interaction and design ideas.
  • Develop high-fidelity mockups and prototypes for web and mobile applications.
  • Conduct user research, interviews, and usability testing to gather insights and validate design decisions.
  • Collaborate with product managers, developers, and other stakeholders to define and implement innovative solutions.
  • Establish and promote design guidelines, best practices, and standards.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest UI/UX trends, techniques, and technologies.

Required Qualifications

  • Proven UI/UX design experience with a strong portfolio showcasing your design process and solutions.
  • Proficiency in design and prototyping tools such as Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, or similar.
  • Solid understanding of user-centered design principles, interaction design, and visual design.
  • Experience designing for responsive web and mobile platforms (iOS and Android).
  • Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in a team environment and manage multiple projects.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for rapid prototyping.
  • Experience with data visualization and analytics.
  • Knowledge of motion design and animation principles.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and performance bonuses.
  • Opportunities for professional development and learning.
  • Flexible work hours and remote/hybrid options.
  • Health and wellness programs.
  • A creative, collaborative, and supportive work environment.
